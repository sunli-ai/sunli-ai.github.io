About Me

I am currently an assistant professor at School of Control and Computer Engineer, North China Electric Power University (NCEPU), Beijing. Before joining in NCEPU, I received my Ph.D. Degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2021 supervised by Prof. Su, Sen . I was a visiting scholar at University of Illinois at Chicago advised by Prof. Philip. S. Yu (ACM/IEEE Fellow) from 2019 to 2020.
My research areas are machine learning and data mining with special attentions to graphs and Riemannian representation learning. I have published over 20 refereed papers, including top conferences and journals such as AAAI, IJCAI, ACM The Web Conf. (WWW), ACM CIKM, IEEE ICDM, IEEE TKDE. I have been invited as program committee member in KDD, WSDM, AAAI, IJCAI, ECAI and conference committee member in IEEE BigData, and served as reviewer in ACM TWEB, IJMLC.
