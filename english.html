Short Bio

Dr. Sun, Li is currently an assistant professor at North China Electric Power University, Beijing. He received the Ph.D. Degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2021 supervised by Prof. Su, Sen, and visited University of Illinois at Chicago advised by Prof. Philip. S. Yu (ACM/IEEE Fellow). His research areas are machine learning and data mining with special attentions to graphs and Riemannian representation learning. He has published in AAAI, IJCAI, WWW, ICDM, CIKM and IEEE TKDE, ACM TWEB. He is the winner of NSFC young scholar fund 2022. He has been invited as program committee in KDD, WSDM, AAAI, IJCAI, ECAI and conference committee in IEEE BigData, and served as reviewer in ACM TWEB, IJMLC.
